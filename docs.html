MIMBAI Documentation

Learn how to use MIMBAI — the AI Platform for Smart Organizations. Generate reports, match B2B partners, analyze finances, and more.

🚀 Quick Start

MIMBAI works directly in your browser — no installation required.

1
Open mimbai.com — the app loads automatically in your browser's language.
2
Select a module from the dropdown at the top — or click one of the 4 generator cards on the welcome screen.
3
Click a card or type your request — MIMBAI explains what it can generate and asks for the information it needs.
4
Receive your generated content — text appears in real time as the AI writes. Use 🔊 to hear the response.

🎤 Voice Input & Output

MIMBAI supports full voice conversation — speak your request and hear the AI respond.

Voice Input (Speech to Text)

Tap the 🎤 microphone button in the chat input. Speak your request — the text appears automatically and sends when you finish speaking.

  • Works on Chrome, Safari (iOS & Mac), Edge
  • Automatically uses your selected language
  • Tap the mic again to cancel

Voice Output (Text to Speech)

Every AI response includes a 🔊 speak button. Tap it to hear the response read aloud in your language. The AI also speaks automatically after each response.

  • Portuguese: voice "Luciana" (Apple) / Google português
  • Norwegian: voice "Nora" / Google norsk
  • Spanish: voice "Mónica" / Google español
  • English: voice "Samantha" / Google US English

❓ FAQ

Why does MIMBAI respond in English even when I write in Portuguese?

MIMBAI is instructed to always respond in the same language you write in. If this happens, try refreshing the page. The language detection is automatic from your browser settings.

How do I get the best results?

Be specific. Instead of "create a report", say "create a monthly sales KPI report for a retail company with 3 stores, showing revenue, units sold, and conversion rate for January-March 2026." The more context you provide, the better the output.

Can I export the generated content?

Yes — select all text in the response and copy to any application. Full PDF export and direct Excel integration is coming in MIMBAI Pro v1.1.

Is there a message limit?

Free plan: 20 messages per day. Pro plan: unlimited. See pricing for details.

🌐 Languages

MIMBAI auto-detects your browser language and switches the interface automatically. Supported languages:

  • English — full interface + AI responses + voice (Samantha)
  • Norwegian (Norsk) — full interface + AI responses + voice (Nora)
  • Portuguese Brazil — full interface + AI responses + voice (Luciana)
  • Spanish — full interface + AI responses + voice (Mónica)

For other languages, MIMBAI defaults to English interface but the AI can still understand and respond in most languages.
