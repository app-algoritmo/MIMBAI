Frequently asked questions

Do I need a credit card to start? +
No. The Free plan requires no credit card. You can explore MIMBAI and generate content without any payment details.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
What AI model powers MIMBAI? +
MIMBAI is powered by Anthropic Claude — one of the most capable and safe AI models available. We use Claude Haiku for fast responses and Claude Sonnet for complex generation tasks.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. Your conversations are not stored permanently on our servers without your consent. The API proxy runs on our secure server in Norway. We never sell your data.
What languages are supported? +
MIMBAI supports English, Norwegian, Portuguese (Brazil) and Spanish fully — including interface, AI responses and voice input/output. The AI can also understand and respond in many other languages.
